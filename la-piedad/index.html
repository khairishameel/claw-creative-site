Our Story

Family. Flavor. Broad Ripple.

La Piedad has been a neighborhood staple in Broad Ripple for years, sitting just steps from the Monon Trail. The Rodriguez family built this place on one simple idea: fresh ingredients, cooked right, every time.

Not everything Mexican has to be spicy. Here, every dish is made with authentic, mild flavors and only the freshest ingredients available. If you want the heat, the hot sauce is always on the table.

Come as you are. Leave full.
