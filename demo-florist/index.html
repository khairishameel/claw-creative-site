Our Story

Rooted in Indianapolis,
grown with intention

Petal & Stem started in 2014 with a small studio on the near-northside and a belief that flowers should feel personal — not generic. We work with local Indiana growers whenever possible and keep our arrangements seasonal and honest.

Every order is handled by our small team. No outsourcing, no shortcuts. Just careful work and flowers that actually look like the photos.
