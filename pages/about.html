About

Built in Indianapolis.
Built for local.

Small shop. Big tools. We build what used to take a five-person agency with code, AI, and a lot less ego.

The story.

Most web design studios charge $3,000+ for a site a small business never really needed, and then charge $150/month forever to "maintain" it. We watched this happen to too many of our friends.

So we built something different. We use AI to handle the grunt work — the 10 hours of copy, the 6 hours of image sourcing, the 4 hours of SEO setup. That lets us charge $100 to $250 flat, ship in under 72 hours, and actually make a living.

Then we realized: the AI we use to build sites is also the AI that can live on your site and answer your customers. That's when Claw Creative stopped being a web design studio and became a digital-operations partner.

Today, we build the site, run your socials, and deploy your custom AI agent. All three, one team, no handoffs. Indianapolis-based, Indianapolis-focused, here to stay.

What we believe

The operating principles.

Local first.

We work with Indianapolis businesses because we understand them — and because the best work comes when you can meet for coffee if something's off.

One price. Forever.

Flat fees, not retainers. You own your site. If you want to take it somewhere else one day, we hand you the files. No lock-in.

Ship fast. Keep shipping.

We'd rather push to production in 48 hours and iterate than spend six weeks in discovery docs. Speed is a feature.

No buzzwords.

We don't leverage synergies. We build websites. We write copy. We run socials. We explain what we're doing in words your grandma would understand.

AI is a multiplier, not a replacement.

Every site we ship, every agent we train, is reviewed by a human. AI lets us work faster — it doesn't let us be sloppy.

Honest money.

If we can't help you, we'll say so. If a cheaper tool works, we'll point you to it. We'd rather skip a sale than burn a customer.

That's what we're here for.
