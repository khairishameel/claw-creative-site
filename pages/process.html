Process

Six steps.
Under a week.

No long meetings. No proposals. No "scope calls." Here's exactly what happens, start to finish.

Day 0 · First contact

Send us your business name

Email, WhatsApp, or contact form. We reply within a few hours with a short list of questions — business name, what you do, target customers, must-have features.

Day 0 · Same day

Quote + timeline

You get back a fixed price (First Claw $249.99, Full Grip $499.99) and a guaranteed turnaround. No proposals, no retainers, no hourly billing.

Day 1 · AI interview (optional)

30-minute discovery call

If you're adding the custom AI agent, we talk for 30 minutes. You answer questions about your business. We record, transcribe, and turn it into your agent's knowledge base.

Day 1–2 · Build

We build. You run your business.

We handle design, copy, code, photos, SEO setup, AI training. You don't babysit us. You don't sit in meetings. You get a "here's your site" link when it's ready.

Day 2–3 · Review

You look. You note changes.

We send you the live preview. You review on your phone, your laptop, your kitchen table. You send back notes. We revise (1 round for First Claw, 2 for Full Grip).

Day 3 · Launch

Live. Indexed. Yours.

Site pushes to your domain, we set up Google Business, we install analytics, we hand over logins. You own everything forever.

Forever · Ongoing

We're a text away.

Menu change? New hours? New service? Text us. Most edits ship same day. If you're on Social Media Management, we post all week and send monthly reports.
