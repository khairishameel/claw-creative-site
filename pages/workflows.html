What it replaces

The work nobody wants to do.

Repetitive, deterministic, soul-crushing. Perfect for code.

Data entry

Move data between systems that don't talk. CSV to CRM. PDF to spreadsheet. Email to database. No copy-paste, no typos.

Web scraping

Pull pricing, listings, reviews, contact data from any site on a schedule. Playwright handles JavaScript, login walls, and anti-bot defenses.

Lead generation

Find prospects, enrich with public data, score by fit, push to your CRM. Wake up to a clean pipeline every morning.

Report assembly

Pull metrics from every tool you use, combine, format, email to stakeholders. Same time every week. No human in the loop.

Inbox triage

Read incoming email, classify, route, draft replies, flag urgent items. Inbox zero is just a workflow.

QA + monitoring

Watch your competitors, check your own site, alert on regressions. Run hourly. Notify only when something matters.

How it works

Manual to autonomous, in two weeks.

Step 1 · Days 1 to 3

Map the workflow

We sit with the person who does this work today. Inputs, outputs, edge cases, decision points. We document every click.

Step 2 · Days 4 to 10

Build and test

Python for the logic. Playwright for the browser work. Your APIs where they exist, scrapers where they don't. We test against real data.

Step 3 · Days 11 to 14

Deploy and instrument

Goes live on a schedule. Logs to a dashboard you can read. Alerts on failure. Reports to your inbox.

Ongoing

Yours to keep

You own the code. We hand over the repo, the credentials, the runbook. Optional monthly support if you want us watching it.
