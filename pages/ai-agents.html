How it works

From interview
to deployed in 48 hours.

Step 1 · Day 1

30-minute interview

We sit down with you (phone, Zoom, in person — your call). We ask about what you do, how you talk to customers, what questions come up the most, and the things only you know.

Step 2 · Day 1–2

Training & tuning

We turn the interview into a custom knowledge base. Your menu, your hours, your policies, your story, your voice. The agent reads all of it and learns to answer like you.

Step 3 · Day 2

Deploy to your site

Goes live as a chat widget on your site. Customers tap it, type a question, get an instant answer. Works on phones, tablets, desktops.

Ongoing

Learning & updates

Menu changes? New hours? New service? Send a text. We update the agent same day. Every conversation makes it smarter.
