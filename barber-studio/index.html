Our Story

More Than a Haircut.
It's an Experience.

The Barber Studio has been a staple of Broad Ripple's grooming scene, built on a simple promise — every client leaves looking and feeling their best. Our barbers don't rush. They listen, they craft, and they take pride in every single cut.

Located at 6323 Guilford Ave, we've built a reputation as the premier spot for men who care about the details. From classic cuts to modern fades, shaves to beard trims — we do it all, and we do it right.
